# Vision

## What iFlot is

iFlot is not a traditional fleet management application.

It is a platform for the operational and administrative layer that connects daily transport execution to monthly financial close in mid-market LATAM logistics.

That layer sits at the intersection of:

- ground logistics operations
- shipment documentation and traceability
- integrated pre-billing and billing
- operational and financial reporting

Existing alternatives do not fully cover this intersection. Fleet management platforms focus on vehicle and asset administration. Telematics platforms focus on location and telemetry. Neither typically covers shipment documentation, operational closing, or integrated billing at a price point accessible to companies operating 50 to 150 vehicles.

## The core problem

Discovery so far suggests that monthly close in mid-market LATAM logistics often depends on fragmented systems and manual reconciliation.

Trips are registered in one place. Shipment documents live in another. Billing happens in a spreadsheet. Reconciliation is done by hand, often days after the fact.

The result is operational friction, billing errors, limited traceability, and no clear audit trail from shipment execution to invoice.

## The core hypothesis

The value proposition of iFlot is not better tracking.

It is closing the loop from trip execution to billing without requiring manual reconciliation outside the system.

## Who it is for

iFlot is intended for companies in ground logistics operating 50 to 150 vehicles in LATAM, typically:

- regional freight carriers
- last-mile and distribution operators
- logistics arms of mid-sized commercial companies

These companies have outgrown manual processes but cannot justify the cost or complexity of enterprise logistics platforms.

## Proof of market

Two legacy customers have been using the original system for over a decade despite its known limitations.

They adopted it, paid for it, and kept using it for years. That is evidence of a real and underserved need, not a speculative opportunity.

## What success looks like

A logistics operator using iFlot can:

- register a shipment document and associate it with a trip without leaving the system
- close a trip operationally and immediately know what is billable
- generate a pre-invoice from operationally closed shipment documents without manual input
- cancel, correct, and reprocess documents within the system
- close the month with reports that are consistent with the actual operational record

No spreadsheets. No manual reconciliation. No vendor intervention for routine tasks.

## Current phase

iFlot is in early-stage discovery and technical foundation.

The immediate priorities are:

- validating domain assumptions with legacy customers
- defining the core domain model
- establishing access control and the operational core as the first delivery increment

The roadmap is intentionally being shaped through discovery and validation with legacy customers.

At this stage, architectural and product decisions are grounded in evidence from the legacy system and discovery sessions rather than assumption.
